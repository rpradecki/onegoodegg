What we look for

You may already be a fit.

We're looking for warm, capable, grounded people who'd welcome the chance to raise a child with real resources and a real support system behind them.

Health & stability

Good general health and a dependable life. We cover the full medical screening — you bring the character and consistency.

Warmth & commitment

A genuine wish to raise a child full-time in a loving, present home. This is supported parenthood, agreed openly on both sides.

Curiosity & drive

Ambition for yourself and the child — academics, athletics, or the arts. You want them, and you, to reach far.

Discretion

Comfort keeping your arrangement private. In return, we protect your name and your family as carefully as theirs.
